Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,Cranberries,and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart carmberries, and creamy brie. It will become you new favorite holiday starter!

Ingredients

    For the Focaccia:
  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil,plus more the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon olive oil,for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necesssary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries
